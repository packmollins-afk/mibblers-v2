About Mibblers

Born in the Bay Area, Mibblers is the original consistently dosed mushroom candy. Every batch is crafted for reliability - so you know exactly what you're getting, every time. Fun, light, and made with care in Northern California.

We started Mibblers because consistency matters. No guessing games, no surprises - just a dependable experience you can trust.

Our Product

Mibblers are mushroom-infused candies designed for those who value precision and quality. Each piece is carefully dosed for a balanced, enjoyable experience - whether you're exploring microdosing or looking for something light and fun.

  • Consistently dosed in every batch
  • Fresh baked in Northern California
  • The original - accept no imitations

Frequently Asked Questions

What makes Mibblers different?

Mibblers pioneered consistent dosing in mushroom candy. We measure precisely so every piece delivers the same experience. Many products on the market vary wildly batch to batch - Mibblers doesn't.

Where are Mibblers made?

All Mibblers are crafted fresh in Northern California, in the San Francisco Bay Area where the brand was born.

How do I order?

Use the order form on this page when Mibblers are available. We'll confirm your order and arrange local delivery.

Are there fake Mibblers?

Unfortunately, yes. As the original Mibblers brand, we've seen imitators pop up. If you're not ordering from mibblers.shop, you're not getting the real thing.
